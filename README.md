<h1>JavaScript On The Job: An Interactive Scrollytelling Website</h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
JavaScript On The Job is my first attempt at creating an interactive scrollytelling website that aims to help users better understand a complex concept. Navigating the website is simple: scroll with your middle mouse button and enjoy the story unfolding before your eyes! By the end of your scrolling, you may pick up a thing or two about JavaScript you didn't know before. Whether you are an experienced coder or a complete beginner, JavaScript On The Job is a fun way to experience JavaScript while interacting with a fun story.

<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
<li>Light and Dark Mode</li>
<li>Smoothscrolling Interface</li>
<li>Progress Tracker</li>
<li>Interactive Scroll Animations</li>
</ul>
